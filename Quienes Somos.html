History

Get to know us more

Samsung was founded by Lee Byung-chul in 1938 as a trading company in Taegu, South Korea. Over the decades, Samsung has evolved and diversified its businesses. During the 1960s, the company entered the electronics and construction industries, marking the beginning of its transformation into a global conglomerate. In 1969, Samsung Electronics was founded and quickly became a leader in the production of home appliances and electronic components.
Throughout the 1980s and 1990s, Samsung continued to expand in technology, developing innovative products and expanding its global presence. In the 2000s, Samsung established itself as a leading mobile device manufacturer, and the Galaxy line of phones became one of the most popular worldwide.

Mission

Our Mission

Samsung's mission is "Inspire the world and create the future." This is reflected in its commitment to creating innovative technologies and products that enrich people's lives and contribute to global social development. Samsung seeks to provide new possibilities and experiences for its consumers around the world, with the aim of improving their quality of life. The company strives to provide high-quality products and services that enable people to realize their dreams and live in a safer, healthier and more connected environment. Additionally, Samsung is dedicated to creating shared value and the well-being of communities, driving economic and social development through its business and social responsibility initiatives.

Vision

The Vision

Samsung's vision is "Leading technological innovation in the digital age." Samsung aspires to be a leading company that defines the future through its innovative technologies and solutions. The company strives to maintain a leading position in the technology sector, developing products and services that improve people's lives and anticipate market needs. Samsung seeks to be a pioneer in new technological areas, such as artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things (IoT) and 5G networks, positioning itself at the forefront of innovation. At the same time, the company is committed to sustainable and responsible practices that protect the environment and promote inclusive and equitable development. Samsung's vision includes creating a future where technology is a positive force that connects people and communities, enabling continuous and sustainable progress.

Objectives

Our objectives are:

Continuous innovation: Develop innovative technologies and products that meet and anticipate consumer needs.
Sustainability: Promote sustainable and responsible practices in all its operations to contribute to the well-being of the planet.
Global expansion: Strengthen its presence in key and emerging markets, maintaining a competitive position in the global arena.
Technology Leadership: Maintain and expand your leadership in key sectors such as electronics, mobile devices and semiconductors.
Social Responsibility: Contribute positively to communities through corporate social responsibility initiatives.

Values

Our Values
